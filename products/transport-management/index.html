General functionality of IT Vectura Transport Management

Master data

Data Mastering, providing information content for business objects participating in transport management processes

Contracts

Data management and control of tariff rates and other relevant contract conditions for automatic calculation of freight expenses

Requirements

Registration and management of transport requirements volume for further route planning, movement and delivery

Planning

Formation of routes manually or using mathematical models according to certain planning strategies

Execution

Tracking of the execution process at all stages of transportation

Calculations

Control of planned and calculation of actual freight costs taking into account the conditions of the contract entered into the system

Reporting

Plan-fact reporting, dispatcher monitor, data integration streams with reporting systems

IT Vectura Transport Management

Licensing and Distribution

Distribution and implementation of software products containing the configuration "Transport Management ITVectura TM" is carried out by license partners of the company LLC "IT VECTURA". There are no mandatory requirements for the presence of certified specialists.

The number of licenses purchased for the use of the configuration "Transport Management ITVectura TM" is determined based on the need:

— the maximum number of simultaneous users with this configuration is determined by the number of licenses purchased

— The maximum number of simultaneous mobile clients with this configuration is also determined by the number of licenses purchased.

— The maximum number of registered unique vehicles in the system is determined by the unique registration number of the vehicle, trailers, and other passive vehicles, which do not require separate licensing.

— The maximum number of registered unique power units of the loading and unloading zone in the system is determined by the number of licenses purchased.

Expansion of the number of automated workstations is achieved by purchasing client licenses for the ITVectura TM Transport Management platform on:

— 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 300, and 500 workstations.

— 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 300, and 500 mobile clients.

— 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 300, and 500 unique vehicles used within one calendar year.

— 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 300, and 500 workstations of unique power units of the loading and unloading zone.

The purchase of software products containing the ITVectura TM Transport Management configuration does not include licenses and cannot be used without the purchase of licenses, the number of which is calculated at the time of purchase, based on the need for the maximum number of simultaneous users, simultaneous mobile clients, the number of registered unique vehicles in the system, the number of registered unique power units of the loading and unloading zone in the system.
